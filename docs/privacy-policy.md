# IgiciroHub Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026

IgiciroHub ("we", "our", "the app") is a coffee price prediction and cooperative marketplace application developed as an academic capstone project at the University of Rwanda.

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** name, email, phone number, and role (cooperative or buyer) when you register.
- **Usage data:** coffee price queries, predictions, marketplace listings, and chat messages you send through the app.
- **Device data:** basic network connectivity status (for offline mode); we do not track precise GPS location unless you grant location permission for optional weather features.

## How we use information

- Authenticate you and provide app features (prices, predictions, marketplace, messaging, alerts).
- Send notifications you opt into (in-app, email, or SMS).
- Improve price prediction models using aggregated, anonymized price history.

## Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is stored on our secure server and is not shared with third parties except:

- **SMS/email providers** (Africa's Talking, SMTP) when you enable those notification channels.
- **Google Gemini** when you use the AI assistant (only the message or photo you submit).

## Data retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.

## Security

Passwords are hashed. API access uses encrypted HTTPS (JWT tokens).

## Contact

Email: support@igicirohub.rw

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
