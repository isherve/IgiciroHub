IgiciroHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

IgiciroHub is a coffee price prediction and cooperative marketplace app (University of Rwanda capstone).

Information we collect

  • Account: name, email, phone, role (cooperative/buyer)
  • Usage: predictions, listings, chat messages, alert preferences
  • Connectivity status for offline mode (no precise GPS unless you allow it)

How we use it

To provide authentication, prices, AI predictions, marketplace, messaging, and notifications you opt into.

Third parties

  • SMS/email providers when enabled (Africa's Talking, SMTP)
  • Google Gemini when you use the AI assistant

We do not sell personal data.

Security

HTTPS, hashed passwords, JWT authentication.
