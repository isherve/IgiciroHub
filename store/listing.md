## Full description
IgiciroHub helps Rwandan coffee cooperatives and buyers make smarter decisions.

**For cooperatives**
- See trending coffee prices (farm-gate, cooperative, export USD)
- AI price predictions with confidence ranges
- List coffee on the marketplace and chat with buyers
- Set price alerts via in-app, email, or SMS

**For buyers**
- Browse cooperative listings by variety and location
- Message sellers directly
- Track price trends and forecasts

**Languages:** English, Kinyarwanda, French

Developed as a University of Rwanda capstone project.
